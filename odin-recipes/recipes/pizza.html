Pizza

description

Pizza is an iconic Italian dish that has become a favorite worldwide. It consists of a flatbread base topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings, then baked in an oven. Variations range from classic Margherita to creative options with unique ingredients like pineapple or truffles.

ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Your choice of toppings (pepperoni, vegetables, etc.)

steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 220°C (430°F).
  2. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the rolled-out pizza dough.
  3. Add a generous layer of mozzarella cheese.
  4. Place your chosen toppings on the pizza.
  5. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and cheese is melted.
  6. Remove from the oven, drizzle with olive oil, and serve hot.
