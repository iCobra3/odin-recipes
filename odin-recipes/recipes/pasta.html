Pasta

description

Pasta is a versatile and beloved Italian dish made from wheat flour dough, usually boiled and served with a variety of sauces. Popular versions include spaghetti, penne, and fettuccine paired with tomato-based, creamy, or pesto sauces.

ingredients

  • Pasta of your choice (spaghetti, penne, etc.)
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sauce or cream-based sauce
  • Grated Parmesan cheese

steps

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water until al dent.
  2. In a separate pan, heat olive oil and saut√© garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add your preferred sauce to the pan and simmer for a few minutes.
  4. Drain the pasta and mix it with the sauce.
  5. Top with grated Parmesan cheese and serve hot.
